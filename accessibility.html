Accessibility Statement

Last updated: September 18, 2025

Our Commitment

BrandForge is built with a deep respect for visibility, dignity, and access. We are committed to ensuring that our platform is usable by everyone, regardless of ability or circumstance.

Design Principles

  • High-contrast visuals and dark mode support
  • Keyboard navigation and screen reader compatibility
  • Responsive layout for mobile and assistive devices
  • Clear language and intuitive interactions

Continuous Improvement

We actively test and refine our interface to meet evolving accessibility standards. Feedback from users is welcomed and valued.

Contact

If you encounter any barriers or need assistance, please reach out to support@brandforge.ai. We will respond promptly and work to resolve any issues.
