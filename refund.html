Refund Policy

Last updated: September 18, 2025

Subscription Refunds

BrandForge subscriptions are billed in advance and are non-refundable once activated. We encourage users to review tier details before subscribing.

Exceptional Circumstances

Refunds may be considered in cases of duplicate billing, technical failure, or unauthorized charges. To request a review, contact support@brandforge.ai within 7 days of the transaction.

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation prevents future billing but does not retroactively refund past charges.

Custom Branding Packages

Due to the personalized nature of branding packages, refunds are not available once generation begins. We aim to ensure satisfaction through clear onboarding and previews.
